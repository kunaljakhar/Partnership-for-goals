About Setu

Bridging the gap between organizations and clients through AI-powered collaboration

Our Mission

Setu is dedicated to achieving UN Sustainable Development Goal 17 by fostering partnerships that accelerate progress towards all SDGs. We leverage artificial intelligence to streamline the collaboration process between organizations and clients.

How It Works

Our platform uses four specialized AI agents to facilitate seamless partnerships:

  • Discovery Agent: Matches clients with relevant projects based on skills and interests
  • Negotiation Agent: Facilitates fair contract terms and validates negotiation ranges
  • Document Agent: Automatically generates legal documents and MoUs
  • Communication Agent: Provides context-aware communication suggestions

Impact

By reducing friction in partnership formation, Setu enables organizations to focus on what matters most - creating positive impact in the world.
